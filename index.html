The Aston Martin DB12 is a grand touring car produced by the British car
manufacturer Aston Martin. The car was unveiled in May 2023
as a replacement for the DB11. Aston Martin has held a royal warrant as purveyor of motorcars
to Charles III (as Prince of Wales and later as King) since 1982,
and has over 160 car dealerships in 53 countries, making it
a global automobile brand. The company is traded on the London
Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index.In 2003
it received the Queen's Award for Enterprise for outstanding contribution
to international trade. The company has survived seven bankruptcies throughout its history.
